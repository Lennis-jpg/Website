Destinations

Discover the world’s most popular travel destinations, where adventure meets culture and unforgettable experiences await.

  • England

    London

    A global city blending history with modernity. Explore iconic landmarks like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace and Big Ben, while enjoying the vibrant culture, theater in the West End and diverse dining scene.

  • Scotland

    Edinburgh

    A captivating city of cobblestone streets and ancient castles. Edinburgh offers a rich history with the Edinburgh Castle, the Royal Mile and the world-famous Edinburgh Festival, set against the backdrop of volcanic hills.

  • Ireland

    Dublin

    Ireland’s charming capital, famous for its lively pubs, historic architecture and literary heritage. Discover the Guinness Storehouse, Trinity College’s Book of Kells and the warm hospitality of the locals.

  • Netherlands

    Amsterdam

    A picturesque city of canals, bikes and art. Amsterdam boasts museums like the Van Gogh Museum and Rijksmuseum, historic buildings and vibrant neighbourhoods perfect for cycling, shopping and dining.

  • Morocco

    Marrakesh

    An enchanting city of rich culture and exotic markets. Wander the vibrant souks, explore the opulent palaces and gardens and experience the magic of the Jemaa el-Fna square with its food stalls and performers.

  • Nigeria

    Lagos

    A bustling metropolis where culture, music and business collide. Lagos is famous for its vibrant nightlife, sandy beaches, art galleries and lively markets, making it a city full of energy and contrasts.

  • Ghana

    Accra

    A lively and colourful city that blends traditional and contemporary culture. Accra offers a vibrant arts scene, beautiful beaches, historic sites like the Cape Coast Castle and warm hospitality from its locals.

  • Egypt

    Cairo

    A city steeped in ancient history, where the past meets the present. Marvel at the Pyramids of Giza, explore the Egyptian Museum and wander the chaotic streets filled with markets, food and local life.

  • India

    New Delhi

    A dynamic city where history, politics and culture converge. Visit iconic sites like the Red Fort, Humayun’s Tomb and the Lotus Temple, while experiencing the bustling markets, street food and modern urban life.

  • China

    Beijing

    A city of imperial history and modern flair. Discover the Forbidden City, the Great Wall and the Temple of Heaven, while enjoying the blend of traditional culture and contemporary growth in China’s capital.

  • South Korea

    Seoul

    A futuristic city with a deep respect for its traditions. Explore palaces like Gyeongbokgung, vibrant shopping districts like Myeongdong and enjoy a mix of modern skyscrapers and ancient temples in this buzzing metropolis.

  • Australia

    Canberra

    The calm and collected capital of Australia, known for its cultural institutions like the National experiences and the Australian War Memorial. Surrounded by beautiful landscapes, it’s a hub for politics, art and natural beauty.

  • Italy

    Rome

    A city where history comes alive. Visit ancient ruins like the Colosseum, the Roman Forum and the Vatican. Enjoy mouth-watering Italian cuisine and wander through charming piazzas and narrow streets.

  • France

    Paris

    The City of Light, known for its romantic atmosphere, iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and world-class art at the Louvre. Explore chic cafes, cobbled streets and historic architecture.

  • Japan

    Kyoto

    A cultural gem with traditional tea houses, serene temples and beautiful gardens. Kyoto offers an authentic taste of Japan's heritage, with picturesque landscapes and stunning autumn foliage.

  • Kenya

    Nairobi

    Known for its impressive skyline and bustling markets, Nairobi offers a unique position with wildlife right on its doorstep. Just a short drive from the city center, you can visit the Nairobi National Park, where giraffes, zebras and lions roam against the backdrop of the city’s skyscrapers.

  • South Africa

    Cape Town

    A coastal city with breathtaking views of Table Mountain, gorgeous beaches and diverse cultural offerings. A mix of adventure, relaxation and history, from the Waterfront to the nearby Winelands.

  • Brazil

    Rio de Janeiro

    Famous for its samba culture, Copacabana Beach and the Christ the Redeemer statue, Rio combines vibrant nightlife, festive carnivals and stunning natural beauty, all surrounded by mountains and ocean.

  • United States of America

    New York City

    The Big Apple. A vibrant, bustling metropolis full of culture, iconic landmarks like Times Square, Central Park and Broadway. There's never a dull moment with world-class museums, shopping and cuisine.

  • Jamaica

    Kingston

    A lively city steeped in reggae culture, Kingston is home to the Bob Marley Museum and vibrant music scenes with its bustling markets, delicious food and nearby Blue Mountains.

  • Greece

    Santorini

    A stunning island in the Aegean Sea, known for its whitewashed buildings, blue-domed churches and breathtaking sunsets. Enjoy charming villages, crystal-clear waters and delicious Greek cuisine.

  • Turkey

    Instanbul

    A city where East meets West, offering a unique blend of history, culture and architecture. Explore the Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque and Grand Bazaar, while savoring Turkish delights and mezze dishes.

  • Thailand

    Bangkok

    A bustling, energetic city with rich history and a vibrant street food scene. Visit the grand temples like Wat Arun and Wat Phra Kaew and experience the nightlife, shopping and the hustle of the busy streets.

  • United Arab Emirates

    Dubai

    Dubai is a dazzling city known for its futuristic skyline, luxury shopping and vibrant culture. Home to iconic landmarks like the Burj Khalifa and Palm Islands, it offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition. Whether you're exploring desert dunes, shopping in world-class malls or enjoying stunning beaches.

  • Kazakhstan

    Nur-Sultan

    A modern and futuristic city that rose from the steppe, Nur-Sultan is a blend of cutting-edge architecture and vast open landscapes. Visit iconic structures like the Bayterek Tower and the Palace of Peace and Reconciliation. The city offers a unique look at Kazakhstan's ambition to grow as a global hub.

  • Portugal

    Lisbon

    With its colorful buildings, cobblestone streets and stunning views over the Tagus River, Lisbon is a coastal gem filled with history and charm. Enjoy the nostalgic tram rides, visit the ancient Belém Tower and dive into the delicious world of Portuguese cuisine, including pastéis de nata.

  • Switzerland

    Bern

    Nestled along the Aare River, Bern boasts a well-preserved medieval old town that’s a UNESCO World Heritage site. Wander its charming streets, visit the Zytglogge clock tower and experience the tranquil vibe of this small but picturesque Swiss capital.

  • Singapore

    Singapore

    A dazzling, ultra-modern city-state where skyscrapers meet lush greenery. Explore futuristic architecture like Gardens by the Bay, indulge in the multicultural food scene and shop in glitzy malls along Orchard Road. Singapore is a perfect mix of nature, innovation and culture.

  • Mexico

    Mexico City

    A vibrant, bustling metropolis rich in history and culture. Visit the ancient ruins of Templo Mayor, explore the art scene in Coyoacán and savor authentic street food, including tacos al pastor. With its mix of modernity and tradition, Mexico City is an exhilarating experience.

  • Poland

    Warsaw

    The heart of Poland’s history and resilience, Warsaw combines modern skyscrapers with historic sites like the Royal Castle and Wilanów Palace. After being almost entirely destroyed in WWII, Warsaw has been beautifully rebuilt, offering visitors a dynamic mix of old and new.

  • Sweden

    Stockholm

    Spread across 14 islands, Stockholm is known for its clean beauty, charming old town (Gamla Stan) and contemporary design. Visit the Vasa Museum, relax in the many parks and enjoy Swedish fika in cozy cafés. It’s a city that effortlessly combines history with a modern touch.

  • Tanzania

    Dodoma

    Though Dar es Salaam still holds economic importance, Dodoma is the official capital of Tanzania. Located inland, it’s a quieter, less-touristy place where visitors can explore Tanzania’s political heart, as well as nearby natural wonders like the Ruins of Gombe Stream National Park.

  • Ethiopia

    Addis Ababa

    Vibrant capital, Addis Ababa, is a hub for culture and history, as well as the political capital of the African Union. Explore the National Museum to see “Lucy,” the world-famous early hominid fossil, or dive into the local coffee culture at one of the city's bustling cafes.
